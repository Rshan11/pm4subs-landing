Why I Started PM4Subs

Running a masonry crew means juggling RFIs, photos, change orders, and estimates across a dozen different apps, emails, and text threads. I got tired of losing track of things that cost me money.

So I started building small tools for my own company - quick header calculators, proposal helpers, and ways to stay organized. They worked. Then other subs started asking for them.
