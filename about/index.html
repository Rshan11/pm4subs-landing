About PM4Subs

Built by a subcontractor who just wanted software that actually works.

Why I Started PM4Subs

After years of running masonry crews and juggling RFIs, photos, change orders, and estimates across emails and texts, I realized something: subcontractors don't have simple tools that fit the way we work.

Everything on the market was too expensive, too slow, or designed by people who have never stepped onto a jobsite. So I started building small tools for my own company - quick header calculators, proposal helpers, and ways to stay organized.

Building Tools That Make Sense

PM4Subs exists because subcontractors deserve software that is:

  • Simple
  • Affordable
  • Fast
  • Built around field work

No complexity. No enterprise junk. Just tools that get the job done.

By Subs, For Subs

Everything on this platform is built with real-world experience - from header rentals to spec analysis to tool tracking. If it doesn't save time on the job, it doesn't go in the product.

Thanks for being part of the journey.
