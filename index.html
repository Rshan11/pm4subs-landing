Who PM4Subs Is For

PM4Subs is made for trade contractors who actually do the work: masonry, concrete, framing, drywall, steel, and other subcontractors who are tired of trying to make "GC software" fit their day-to-day. If you run crews, chase change orders, and juggle multiple jobs at once, PM4Subs is built for you.

Why Subs Need Their Own System

Most project management tools are built for general contractors. They're heavy, expensive, and designed around RFIs, drawing logs, and owner meetings. Subcontractors live in a different world: quotes, purchase orders, manpower, tools, small change orders, and keeping foremen on the same page. PM4Subs focuses on what subs actually need instead of burying you in features you'll never use.

How is PM4Subs different from Procore or Buildertrend?

Procore and similar systems are built for general contractors and owners. They're powerful, but often too heavy and expensive for subs. PM4Subs focuses on what subcontractors actually need: clear job tracking, spec review, simple field tools, and staying organized without full-time office staff.
